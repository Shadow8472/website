This is Accrescent

A novel Android app store focused on security, privacy, and usability. Open-source forever.

About

Accrescent is a private and secure Android app store built for the modern era. It aims to provide a developer-friendly platform and pleasant user experience while enforcing modern security and privacy practices and offering robust validity guarantees for installed apps.

Features

  • App signing key pinning: first-time app installs are verified so you don't have to TOFU.
  • Signed repository metadata: even if our servers are compromised, your apps won't be.
  • Automatic, unattended, unprivileged updates: updates are handled seamlessly without breaking the Android security model.
  • Full support for split APKs: downloads are as fast as can be.
  • No remote APK signing: developers are in full control of their app signing keys.
